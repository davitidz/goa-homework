Welcome to gino paradise Tbilisi

Gino Paradise is the ultimate water and wellness destination in the heart of Tbilisi,
Georgia. As one of the largest and most modern aqua parks in the region, it offers a wide
range of activities for visitors of all ages. Whether you're looking for a fun day out with the
family, an exciting ride down a waterslide, or a peaceful escape in the
spa zone, Gino Paradise has something for everyone.

Fun, Relaxation, and Adventure in One Place

The water park features thrilling slides, wave pools, and a lazy river,
all designed to provide maximum excitement and enjoyment. For those seeking
relaxation, the wellness zone offers a variety of saunas, jacuzzis, and massage treatments.
Fitness enthusiasts can take advantage of the gym and swimming pools, while children can enjoy
safe and engaging areas made just for them.

Plan Your Visit

Located near the Tbilisi Sea, Gino Paradise is easily accessible by car and public transport.
The park operates year-round, with both indoor and outdoor areas open during different seasons.
